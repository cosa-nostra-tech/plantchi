# Plantchi Assets

This directory will contain pixel art sprites for the Plantchi tamagotchi characters.

## Planned for Week 3:
- `sprites/happy.png` — 64×64px pixel art happy plant
- `sprites/thriving.png` — glowing plant
- `sprites/thirsty.png` — drooping plant
- `sprites/drowning.png` — overwatered plant (dark)
- `sprites/hungry.png` — pale plant
- `sprites/cold.png` — frost-covered plant
- `sprites/hot.png` — wilting in heat
- `sprites/dim.png` — plant in shadow
- `sprites/scorched.png` — sun-bleached plant
- `sprites/sleeping.png` — plant at night

## Current MVP
The `PlantCharacter` component uses large emoji + text labels as placeholders.
Replace the emoji in `src/components/PlantCharacter.tsx` with `<Image>` components once sprites are ready.
